Pizza Sauce

1 28oz can crushed tomatoes, drained
(puree diced, whole, or fresh tomatoes in blender or
food processor)
1 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
½ tsp salt
½ tp pepper
1 tsp oregano
½ tsp sugar

In a medium saucepan over low heat, saute garlic in
olive oil until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Add
tomatoes, increase heat to medium, and cook until
slightly thickened, 10 to 15 minutes. Season with salt,
pepper, oregano, and sugar.
